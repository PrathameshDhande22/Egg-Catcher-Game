1. Use Left and Right arrow to Move 
    the Basket.

2. You will get only 3 lives for 
    Playing Game.

3. You can Pause the game by Pressing
    the space bar.

4. You can return to the game by 
    pressing the enter button while in 
    paused.

5. If game is over press 1 to play 
    again.

6. If game is over press 2 to go to main 
    menu.

7. If game is over press 3 to exit.

8. Catch the eggs in the basket as many 
    as you can and break the highscore.

9. If you miss to catch the egg then your 
    1 live will get over.
